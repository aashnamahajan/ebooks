WHAT IS E-BOOKS ?

Welcome to E-BOOKS !
E-Books is an app containing thousands of popular titles. These eBooks can be read by all, regardless of the age. Anyone can access these eBooks, which include some of the most popular works of the present and past, using this eBooks app , people can read as well as buy the various books. The goal of E-Books is to encourage a love of reading and serve as a gateway to children reading even more often, whether in school, at libraries, or through other eBook reading apps. People can never get bored when they have these online books along with them. They can read it whenever they want, wherever they want.
